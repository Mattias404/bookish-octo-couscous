Our Areas

The Sheffield Students' Union has all sorts of facillities ranging from food & drink to a nightclub!

The SU contains a wide array of areas for students to use to aid them during their studies.

One of the first areas you’d see after entering the SU is its mini shopping centre, from pot noodles to save for later from University of Sheffield planners you can find it here!

Fancy supporting the university by using a select choice of apperiel. The SU has you covered with its clothes store. Here we offer hoodies, caps t shirts, the lot. All clothes are designed to show of your passion for the university whilst staying in style. The most popular among all has got to be any of the comfy hoodies with “The University of Sheffield” written in an eye catchingly designed and artistic font.

If you feel like doing some shopping without feeling guilty about harming mother nature? Our SU comes with the solution in the form of our zero waste shop. This contains a pic n mix of various natural nuts and nibbles as well as packing basic house supplies such as soap and laundry detergent. This shop only uses sustainable bags for packaging to ensure that the SU and Sheffield stays green.

Eating Options

When hungry, the SU becomes a nexus of eating options available to devour, regardless of your preferences/diet. In the heart of the Unions first floor you will find a cafeteria with a multitude of stalls which you can buy food from if you haven’t been offered free food upon entering the SU. The food here ranges from giant pizza slices to custom wraps so you can be assured that you can find something delicious for a good price since it’s designed for students and you can save money using your GeniUS card here for extra points with each item eaten.

If you feel like having a meal you can consume with a straw you can check out Pearls. This is where you can find some glorious bubble tea rich with antioxidants. The menu is both large in volume and variance with a vast selection of toppings to make your tea feel personal.
