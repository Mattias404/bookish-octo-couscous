About Us

Sheffield Students' Union has been voted #1 SU in the UK

The Students Union provides a location for students of the university to do all sorts of things: work, relax, eat … the list is a lot longer that this. This website is designed to give a guide for students (in Computer Science in particular) from a students point of view. Hopefully whether you’re using a desktop pc or mobile device, these web pages should be displayed in a responsive way so you can enjoy and learn all about the SU with ease.
